About us

VERONIA is a Tunisian Startup founded by a team of multidisciplinary engineers from different prestigious engineering schools. VERONIA offers computing solutions for the industry, (including food industry), Agriculture, the service companies, the startups, the associations, the clubs, the organizations... This startup, the name of which is inspired by a beautiful resistant flower endowed with a very big capacity of adaptation, offers the services of custom-made intelligent solutions adapted to your objectives and designed to satisfy your needs

Our vision: VERONIA dreams about a world, where the services of IT, IOT, and advanced technologies Integrate all the domains to facilitate the life of the humanity. We dream about a world, where the agriculture becomes computerized and intelligent, where the farmer use his smart device to optimize the agricultural production in order to ensure durability and a reasonable use of natural resources..
